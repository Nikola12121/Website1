Powerful Performance and Exceptional Value for Money

The Poco X3 Pro is an incredibly powerful and affordable smartphone. With the Snapdragon 860 chipset and Adreno 640 GPU, it delivers outstanding speed and performance. The large 6.67-inch IPS LCD display with Full HD+ resolution and a 120Hz refresh rate provides vibrant Design and a smooth browsing experience. With a 48MP quad-camera setup and a 20MP selfie camera, you can capture every moment with detail. The 5,160mAh battery lasts all day, and the support for fast charging enables quick top-ups. The Poco X3 Pro offers exceptional value for money and is an ideal choice for those who want a powerful and affordable smartphone.
